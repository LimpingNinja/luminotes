supported browsers

Luminotes makes use of some advanced browser features, so not all browsers will work for editing your wiki. Supported browsers include:

  • Internet Explorer 6+
  • Firefox 1.5+

The following web browsers are known not to work with some or all Luminotes features, at least currently:

  • Safari
  • Chrome
  • Opera
  • Konqueror
  • Lynx

Want Luminotes to support your browser? See my contact info and cast your vote. The more people who ask me to support a certain browser, the more likely I'll add support for that browser in an upcoming release.
