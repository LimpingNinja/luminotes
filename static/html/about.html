about

Luminotes is a personal wiki notebook for organizing your notes and ideas. It's designed for:

  • writers
    Keep track of your characters and storylines.
  • students
    Take notes and make links between related concepts.
  • game designers
    Build your world, one detail at a time.
  • creative types
    If you've outgrown a Word doc or text file full of disorganized notes, Luminotes is for you.
